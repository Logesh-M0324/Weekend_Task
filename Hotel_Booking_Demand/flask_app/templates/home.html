📊 Platform Overview

This dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of hotel booking performance by consolidating key operational, customer, revenue, and statistical metrics into a single interface for business monitoring and decision-making.

Key Highlights
  • Real-time monitoring of booking performance.
  • Integrated customer, revenue, and cancellation analytics.
📈 Booking Trends

Monthly booking trends and booking status provide a quick overview of reservation activity, occupancy patterns, and cancellation distribution throughout the reporting period.

Key Highlights
  • Seasonal demand significantly influences booking volume.
  • Booking status helps evaluate operational performance.
💡 Business Insights

Executive KPIs summarize important business indicators including booking demand, customer retention, hotel performance, revenue generation, and lead time.

Key Highlights
  • Lead time supports demand forecasting.
  • Repeat guests indicate customer loyalty.
📌 Executive Summary
  • The dashboard consolidates hotel booking analytics into a single decision-support platform.
  • Executive KPIs provide a quick snapshot of operational performance.
  • Interactive visualizations simplify trend analysis and business monitoring.
  • Integrated analytics support data-driven decisions across bookings, customers, revenue, and cancellations.
  • The platform serves as a centralized business intelligence solution for hotel performance analysis.
