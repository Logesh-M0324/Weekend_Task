❌ Overall Cancellation Status

🏨 Cancellation by Hotel Type

This visualization compares cancellations across City Hotels and Resort Hotels to identify which property type experiences greater booking losses.

Business Insight
  • Hotels with higher cancellation rates require targeted retention strategies.
  • Property-specific cancellation analysis supports better operational planning.
📊 Market & Deposit Analysis

Market segment and deposit type significantly influence cancellation behavior. Certain booking channels and payment policies are associated with higher cancellation probabilities.

Business Insight
  • Flexible booking policies may increase cancellations.
  • Deposit requirements can help reduce last-minute booking losses.
📌 Executive Summary
  • Cancellation behavior directly impacts hotel occupancy and revenue.
  • Lead time is an important factor influencing booking cancellations.
  • Hotel type, booking channel, and deposit policy all contribute to cancellation trends.
  • Understanding these patterns enables better forecasting and operational planning.
  • Targeted pricing and booking policies can reduce cancellation-related losses.
